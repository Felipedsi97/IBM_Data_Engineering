# Sales & Service Dashboard Analysis in IBM Cognos Analytics

## Overview
This repository contains two dashboards that analyze **Sales Performance** and **Service Quality**, focusing on profitability, volume, customer sentiment, and product reliability.

---

## Sales Dashboard

### 1. Profit (KPI)
Displays the total profit generated across all dealers and models, highlighting overall financial performance.

### 2. Quantity Sold (KPI)
Shows the total number of units sold, providing a high-level view of sales volume.

### 3. Quantity Sold by Model
Compares sales volume across different vehicle models, identifying top- and low-performing products.

### 4. Profit by Dealer ID
Illustrates profit contribution by each dealer, helping identify the most and least profitable dealers.

---

## Service Dashboard

### 1. Number of Recalls by Model
Shows the total number of recalls per vehicle model, indicating relative product reliability.

### 2. Customer Sentiment Hierarchy
Breaks down customer feedback into positive, neutral, and negative sentiment to assess overall satisfaction.

### 3. Profit and Quantity Sold by Month
Tracks monthly sales volume alongside profit, revealing seasonality and margin variations.

### 4. Recalls by Model and Affected System
Heatmap highlighting recall intensity across vehicle systems and models, identifying recurring quality issues.
